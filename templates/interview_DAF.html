Understanding the DAF (Detailed Application Form)

The Detailed Application Form (DAF) is not just a form ‚Äî it's your personal blueprint that guides the UPSC interview board. Once you qualify the Preliminary Exam, you are required to fill the DAF for the Mains and Interview stage. It contains crucial information about your background, interests, education, and preferences. A well-presented DAF can set the tone for your interview and help you stand out positively.
